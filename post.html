I lived in Saudi Arabia for 12 years, and while I was there I fell in love with the culture, food, people, and the other countries in the Middle East.

Here are some of my favorite places and memories from my time there:


  1. The Empty Quarter
    • 8 hours away from civilization and desert for miles
    • Lots of shark teeth and arrowheads
    • Great place to go dirt biking

  2. Dubai
    • Home of the Burj Khalifa
    • Unique Architecture
    • Luxurious and Desert Experience

  3. Saudi Arabia
    • The Red Sea - Great for Snorkeling and Scuba Diving
    • Edge of the World
    • AlUla
